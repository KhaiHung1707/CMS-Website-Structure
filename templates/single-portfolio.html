// The challenge

A beautiful site that couldn't convert.

Luma's legacy storefront looked the part but buckled on mobile — 4-second loads, a four-step checkout, and product pages search engines barely indexed.

Conversion sat at 1.2% on mobile while 70% of traffic came from phones. Every redesign attempt had bolted features onto the same slow foundation.

// Our approach

Rebuilt on a typed foundation.

We moved Luma to a headless, component-driven stack — a typed front end on a fast commerce API, with a design system so the team could ship without us.

Checkout collapsed to one page. Images, fonts, and scripts were put on a strict performance budget. Product schema and clean crawl paths were engineered in, not added later.

Go-live was zero-downtime during peak season, with monitoring on Core Web Vitals from day one.
