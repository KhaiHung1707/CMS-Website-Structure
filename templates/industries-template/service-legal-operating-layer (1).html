// The automation engine

Nine automations that run your firm while you practice.

Not a form-builder. A connected automation engine that routes, generates, tracks, and acts on its own — on top of the software you already run. Scroll to see each one work.

// 01 · auto-triage

Sorts and routes every inquiry

The intake form's own answers tag, score, and route each inquiry to the right person — and fire an instant reply, 24/7.

// 02 · sequences

Follow-ups that fire on behaviour

No-show, stalled documents, an unconverted lead — each triggers its own sequence automatically. Nothing depends on someone remembering.

// 03 · workflow engine

"When this happens, do that"

Build multi-step, conditional rules across the whole firm. One event sets off a chain of tasks, emails, and updates on its own.

// 04 · blueprints

A whole matter, spun up in one click

Pick a matter type and the full task list, deadlines, documents, and assignments generate themselves — no rebuilding from scratch.

// 05 · deadlines

Critical dates calculated for you

The engine works out every deadline by matter type and jurisdiction, sets reminders, and escalates before anything is missed.

// 06 · document automation

Documents that write themselves

Letters, agreements, and government forms generate from matter data in seconds. Every field filled, nothing retyped.

// 07 · document intake

Every upload checked and filed

Each file is matched to the matter's required list, filed automatically, and anything still missing is chased for you.

// 08 · integrations

Every tool kept in sync, two ways

Your PMS, accounting, calendar, e-sign, and payments stay in lockstep. Data flows once and lands everywhere it belongs.

// 09 · analytics

It spots the bottleneck before you do

Live dashboards surface where matters stall, your realization rate, and which channels actually bring signed clients.
